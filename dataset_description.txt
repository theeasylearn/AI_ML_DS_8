## 1. anagrams

**Description:** Word puzzle experiment dataset measuring accuracy and solving performance under different conditions.

**Common Fields:**

* subidr
* attnr
* num1
* num2
* num3

**Plots You Can Create:**

* Box Plot (performance comparison)
* Bar Plot (average score by group)
* Violin Plot (distribution analysis)
* Histogram

---

**Fields:**

**Plots You Can Create:**
